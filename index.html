Capstone Research · GIS Spatial Analysis
Industrial Cities Spatial Analysis — Dammam

A spatiotemporal study of three industrial cities (D1, D2, D3) in Dammam, examining 25 years of industrial development across 120 factory records and nine industry classifications.

Objective Analyze the spatial growth and industrial distribution patterns across three industrial cities over a 25-year period to identify development trends.
Methodology Applied multiple GIS analytical methods including Kernel Density Estimation, Location Quotient, Weighted Overlay, Buffer Analysis, Near Tool, Sensitivity Analysis, and Density per Area.
Findings Revealed a complementary — not competing — industrial ecosystem across D1, D2, and D3, with each city developing a distinct industrial specialization over time.
